SOCIAL NAME


WHAT IS SOCIAL NAME?

The social name is the name of the transgenders and how they identify themselves. It is how they choose to be called, accordingly to their gender identity. It represents who the citizen is and it reflects your gender identity.
Social name recognition is a right conquered by trans people, who daily face the embarrassment of being called by a name that represents a gender with which they don’t identify.

SOCIAL NAME IN THE WORK ENVIRONMENT

There isn’t a law that obliges companies to adopt their associates' social names, but when denying the usage the company lets go of any effort of promoting diversity and inclusion in its environment.
Although there isn’t a law, the Public Ministry of Labor posted a new technic that guides the embracement of the social name and says that the non-embracement is a regression and the clear promotion of inequality and prejudice against trans people.

SOCIAL NAME AT SCHOOL

In January 2018, the Education Ministry officially authorized the use of trans people’s social names on the schools' registry. The right of choosing the name that better represents your identity in the school environment is considered an achievement to the community, but still faces lots of obstacles in practice.
The IBTE (Brazilian Trans Institute of Education) created a platform to monitor and receive complaints from young people who had their social names recused by their schools. A survey done between 2018 and 2019 registered 50 complaints, the majority were made by trans men.
