HOW TO CHANGE YOUR LEGAL NAME


Altering your legal name became possible without gender redesignation surgery orcourt decision in the year 2018, but the process is still bureaucratic and expansive.

To do the change is necessary to present your birth certificate, a copy of your ID, a copy of your social security number, voter registration, proof of residence, and a marriage certificate. To duplicate your birth certificate can be solicited on the civil registry, preferably in the unit the person was registered, where the price of the document is lowered, once it is not necessary to cover the transporting taxes between offices.

Besides the documents, a list of certificates is solicited, like Electoral Justice ofthe person's place of residence in the last five years; certification from the civil distributor of the place of residence for the last five years (state/federal); certificate from the criminal distributor of the place of residence for the last five years (state/federal); criminal execution certificate from the place of residence for the last five years (state/federal); certificate from the Labor Court of the place of residence for the last five years; certificate from the Military Justice; and certification from the notary offices of protests in the place of residence for the last five years.

To correct the birth certificate's name and/or gender, it is necessary to submit all documents and certificates to the registry office. The rectification can be requested at any registry office, but it will be carried out in the unit where the person was registered for the first time.
